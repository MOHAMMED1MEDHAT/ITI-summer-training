Information Technology Institute (ITI) is a national institution founded by the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) in Egypt. ITI aims to provide Egyptian youth with the technical and soft skills needed to be employed in the ICT industry. ITI is committed to playing an active role in supporting the Egyptian society and economy by providing the best technical education and training to prepare a highly qualified professional workforce to the ICT industry in Egypt and abroad.
